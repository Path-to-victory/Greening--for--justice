Why Protecting the Environment Matters

Protecting the environment is important for the survival of all living organisms. Human activities such as deforestation, pollution and excessive waste production have negatively affected the planet.

Simple Ways to Keep the Environment Green

  • Plant trees and protect forests.
  • Reduce, reuse and recycle waste.
  • Use renewable energy sources.
  • Avoid plastic pollution.
  • Educate communities about environmental protection.
