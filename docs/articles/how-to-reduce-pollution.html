Introduction

Pollution is one of the biggest threats to the environment today. It affects air, water, soil, and the health of living organisms. Human activities such as burning fossil fuels, improper waste disposal, and industrial emissions are major contributors to pollution.

Ways to Reduce Pollution

  • Reduce the use of plastic materials.
  • Practice recycling and proper waste disposal.
  • Plant more trees to absorb carbon dioxide.
  • Use renewable energy sources like solar and wind.
  • Encourage environmental education in communities.

Conclusion

Reducing pollution requires collective action from individuals, communities, organizations, and governments. Small daily actions can create a significant impact in protecting the environment for future generations.
